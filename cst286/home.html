CST286 · Physics of Computing (with AI)

Build a Computer From the Physics Up

From charge and motion, to logic gates, to a machine you build yourself.
Each sprint climbs a level of abstraction — the physics of the physical world, the logic that runs on it, and the computer you build from a single gate — with AI as your thinking partner the whole way.
Computing Talent
Initiative

A two-week orientation eases you in. Then three core sprints, each climbing a level of abstraction. A two-week synthesis to prove it.
