Professional Seminar

Becoming a Self-Directed Learner.

Technical skills get you hired. Self-directed learning keeps you relevant.

Each sprint builds on the last — from identifying your gaps, to developing skills with accountability, to reading your field's future, to proving your growth with evidence. The portfolio you defend at the end isn't a collection of assignments — it's a record of how you changed.
