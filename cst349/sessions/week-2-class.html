Sprint 1 Philosophy

Why "Beyond Technical"?

"What skill gap is actually holding me back ‚Äî and can I be honest enough to name it?"

Why This Matters:

What We Observed

Patterns from Your Submissions

Three common ways we avoid the uncomfortable truth.

Pattern A: The Catch-All

"Communication" appears in almost every submission. It's safe, vague, and masks what's really happening. What KIND of communication? In what context? With whom?

Pattern B: Focus on What You Control

"My teammates didn't communicate well" or "The project had unclear requirements." These may be true ‚Äî but what could YOU have done differently? Find the part you had control over.

Pattern C: The Safe Choice

Picking something that's easy to admit, socially acceptable, or already improving. If it doesn't sting a little to write down, it's probably not the real gap.

The Reframe

From Surface to Substance

What does it look like to go deeper?

Surface Answer What's Underneath Reframed (YOU as protagonist)
"Communication" I avoid difficult conversations because I fear conflict "I back down when I sense disagreement, even when I'm right"
"Time management" I say yes to everything because I want to be liked "I over-commit because saying no feels like rejection"
"Teammates didn't communicate" I had opportunities to follow up or address it directly "I could have checked in earlier or raised the issue ‚Äî that's what I control"

The reframe puts YOU at the center. Not as blame ‚Äî as agency.

The Tool

5 Whys: Going Deeper

A simple technique that forces you past surface explanations.

üîç How It Works

Start with your skill gap. Ask "Why?" Then ask "Why?" to that answer. Keep going until you hit something that feels true ‚Äî usually around the 4th or 5th why.

Example:

Gap: "I struggle with public speaking"

‚Üí Why? "I get nervous in front of groups"

‚Üí Why? "I worry about being judged"

‚Üí Why? "I equate my ideas with my worth"

‚Üí Why? "Criticism of my ideas feels like criticism of me"

‚Üí Root: "I haven't learned to separate my identity from my output"
