Sprint 1 Demonstration

Handwritten Reflection

60 minutes total. No notes, no AI, no references. Just what you know.

30:00

Part 1: Write (30 min)

Answer the required question + choose 1 of 2. Handwritten, two sides of a page.

Required:

"Walk through your 5 Whys chain. At which 'why' did you identify something you can actually change — a root cause where you have agency to make progress?"

Choose 1 of 2:

Option A:

"On a scale of 1-10, how much progress did you actually make? Defend your rating with specific evidence. What would move you one point higher?"

Option B:

"Describe something you tried that didn't work. Why didn't it work? What did that failure teach you about yourself or your approach?"

Part 2: Partner Review (30 min)

  1. Swap papers with your partner
  2. Read silently. Then discuss: Is the evidence convincing?
  3. Your partner types up your answers and submits on Canvas

What Changes Now

From Solo to Triad

Sprint 1

You developed a skill on your own. Nobody was watching. The only accountability was yourself.

Sprint 2

You'll work in a triad. External accountability. Your partners will see your progress — and you'll see theirs.

Can you maintain your growth when someone else is watching? That's the Sprint 2 question.

Concept Spine: LLM Intuition

What AI Can't Do

Recognizing genuine human learning.

AI can generate a reflection that sounds convincing. It can write about "growth" and "learning" all day.

What it cannot do is demonstrate that growth actually happened.

AI can produce

Polished language, general insights, plausible-sounding reflections about any topic

Only you can produce

Specific evidence, honest self-assessment, real behavioral change that others can verify

Your triad partners will know the difference. So will you.

Looking Ahead — March 11

Sprint 2 Demo Questions

These are the questions you'll answer at the end of Sprint 2. Start thinking about them now.

The questions you're working toward:

  • "What did accountability from your triad change about how you approached your skill development?" — Be specific about what shifted.
  • "What evidence of growth can you point to that your triad partners would confirm?" — Not just your claim. Evidence others observed.
  • "Where did external feedback differ from your self-assessment? What did that reveal?"

Sprint 1 tested self-awareness. Sprint 2 tests whether external accountability makes you better.
