Skill

Asking Better Questions

Strengthening our ability to ask good questions given some information is a foundational skill in the age of AI, whether you are talking to a human or working with AI. The quality of those questions will determine how useful the conversation is — for both of you and what value you are bringing to the table.

QFT Rules

1. Write as many questions as you can

2. Do not stop to discuss, judge, or answer any question

3. Write every question exactly as it comes to you

4. Change any statements into questions

Closed

"Did your information diet actually change during Sprint 3?"

Yes/no

Open

"What is the most surprising thing you started consuming that you never would have before this sprint?"

Has to explain

Neither is better. Closed questions establish facts. Open questions explore reasoning. Tag each: (O) or (C). Circle your priority question.

Practice

Demo Prep Activity

25 minutes total, approximately 12.5 min per person. Use your judgement on pacing.

1. Think about your responses

2. Pair up — Partner A shares, Partner B listens and notes question triggers

3. Partner B writes 3-4 QFT questions, tags O/C, circles priority

4. Partner B asks questions, Partner A responds

5. Switch roles — compressed

6. Set 1-2 specific goals for strengthening responses before Demo Day
