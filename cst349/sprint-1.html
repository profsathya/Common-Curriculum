Sprint 1: Beyond Technical

"Why broader skills matter" — Identifying and developing your soft skill gap

Weeks 1-4 Scaffolding: High

By Sprint End, You Will Have:

  • Identified a meaningful soft skill gap through honest self-assessment
  • Applied 5 Whys to find the root cause behind the gap
  • Created a 4-week development plan targeting one skill
  • Demonstrated skill development with evidence
  • Calibrated self-perception against peer perception

Grading

Goal Setting (30%) — Self-assessment and development plan
Productive Reflections (30%) — Weekly reflections showing growth
Demonstration (40%) — Evidence of skill development
