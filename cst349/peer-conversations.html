Why This Matters

Your career will depend more on who knows your capabilities than on your GPA. These conversations are practice for informational interviews, team collaboration, and professional relationships.

The structure gives you permission to initiate conversations you might otherwise avoid. It replaces the uncertainty of "should I approach this person?" with clear expectations that make connection the norm.

"Deep partnerships"‚Äîpeople you talk with 3+ times‚Äîbecome references, collaborators, people who actually know your work. "Broad connections"‚Äî1-2 conversations‚Äîexpand your perspective and network reach.

How It Works

  1. Have a conversation
    During class time or outside of class, have a substantive conversation with a peer using the week's prompt.
  2. Both partners submit the form
    After the conversation, both you and your partner open the Google Form on your phone and submit (takes ~2 minutes).
  3. System cross-references submissions
    Both partners must submit within 48 hours for the conversation to count. This verifies both people engaged.
  4. Receive your updated status
    You'll get an email showing your sprint progress, semester progress, network visualization, and suggested next actions.
