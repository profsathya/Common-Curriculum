Week 14: Review — Pressure-Test Your Evidence

UMPIRE phase: Review

Defending your work means more than presenting it. You need to anticipate tough questions, acknowledge weaknesses before others point them out, and stay composed when challenged. Mock defenses with peers prepare you for the real thing.

Know the Difference

STRONG DEFENSE

"My weakest area is probably Review — I kept skipping it in Sprint 2. But by Sprint 3, I built a check-in habit. Here's the evidence of that shift..."

WEAK DEFENSE

"I didn't have time to develop that area. The assignment was confusing. I think I did fine on everything..."

Strong defense acknowledges weakness and pivots to learning. Weak defense makes excuses.

Portfolio Defense Format

Duration: 15–20 minutes (10 min presentation, 5–10 min Q&A)
Format: One-on-one with instructor
Evidence: Physical notebook + digital portfolio artifacts
Focus: Tell the story of how your thinking changed — using the UMPIRE phases as your evidence structure

Your Defense Should Show Growth Across the UMPIRE Cycle

U — How you identify what you don't know
M — How you survey before you act
P — How you create actionable plans
I — How you execute and notice when plans break
R — How you distinguish activity from progress
E — How you learn about your own learning
