Implement your defense. Review your evidence. Evaluate your growth.

By Sprint End, You Will Be Able To:

Defend your value without being defensive.

Support claims with evidence. Acknowledge weaknesses before others point them out. Stay composed when challenged.

The I–R–E Arc

Sprints 1–3 built the first half of the UMPIRE cycle: you learned to Understand your gaps, Map your landscape, and Plan your approach. This sprint completes the cycle. You'll Implement your portfolio defense, Review your evidence honestly, and Evaluate what changed about how you learn.

I — Implement R — Review E — Evaluate Interview Skill: Defend Your Work

Portfolio Defense Format

Duration: 15–20 minutes (10 min presentation, 5–10 min Q&A)

Format: One-on-one with instructor

Evidence: Physical notebook + digital portfolio artifacts

Focus: Tell the story of how your thinking changed — using the UMPIRE phases as your evidence structure

Your Defense Should Show Growth Across the UMPIRE Cycle

U — How you identify what you don't know
M — How you survey before you act
P — How you create actionable plans
I — How you execute and notice when plans break
R — How you distinguish activity from progress
E — How you learn about your own learning
