The Capability: Self-Directed Learning

Techniques and habits combine into one observable capability ‚Äî the meta-skill that makes all other learning possible.

SDL: Self-Directed Learner

The ability to identify what you need to learn, figure out how to learn it, and actually do the learning ‚Äî without waiting for someone to teach you. This is the meta-skill that determines your adaptability and value in an AI-transformed economy.

SDL isn't about learning alone. It's about directing your learning ‚Äî choosing what to pursue, how to pursue it, and knowing when you've succeeded. You'll use AI, peers, and instructors as resources, but you drive the process.

Built from: Reading + Writing + Talking + Listening + Slow Down + Know Yourself + Take the Lead
