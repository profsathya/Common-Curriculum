How this works · Read first

The Look-back workflow — four steps.

  1. Open your existing "Sprint 4 Look-backs" Google Doc. Add a new section titled "Look-back 4 — Information Diet Audit".
  2. Open your original Sprint 3 Information Diet Audit in a new tab and re-read it before you write. Open it here →
  3. Write your responses to the 6 prompts below, directly in your Look-back doc.
  4. Export as PDF and submit here on Canvas. File → Download → PDF Document. Each submission is the full Look-back doc to date.

The 6 prompts

Step 1 of 6

What surprised you re-reading the Audit?

What's true on the page that you forgot you'd written?

Step 2 of 6

What was the Audit trying to develop in you?

Name the actual capability — not "track what you read."

Step 3 of 6

What would a genuine attempt have looked like for you, specifically?

What would honest documentation of your actual consumption have looked like, including the parts you didn't want to write down?

Step 4 of 6

The wrong way — and whether you took it.

Did you write the document you wished was true, or the document of what was actually true?

Step 5 of 6

Did anything change in your information diet between Sprint 3 and now?

Be honest — yes, partially, no. Cite specific evidence either way.

Step 6 of 6

One sentence about what you actually consume that shapes your thinking.

Not what you wish shaped your thinking. The honest sentence about what's actually doing the work.
