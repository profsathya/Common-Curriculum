Demonstration with Evidence

Sprint 2 Demo Day · Wednesday, March 11 (in class)

This is your Sprint 2 demo — presented to your triad group in class. Unlike Sprint 1's handwritten demo, this one uses 5 slides to tell a growth story backed by evidence.

Your group partners will evaluate whether your evidence is convincing. This is 40% of your Sprint 2 grade.

Your 5-Slide Presentation:

  1. The quality & why it matters — What skill and why?
  2. Your starting point — Where were you at the start of Sprint 2?
  3. What you did — Specific actions with details
  4. Evidence of growth — Before/after, external feedback, concrete examples
  5. Your trajectory / what's next — Honest assessment and direction

What Makes a Strong Demo

Your group partners will be evaluating you on:

  • Specificity: Concrete examples, not vague claims
  • Evidence quality: Before/after comparisons, external validation, measurable change
  • Honesty: Acknowledging what didn't work, not just successes
  • Growth narrative: A clear arc from starting point to current state to future direction

Demo Day Logistics

  • 5 slides maximum — no exceptions
  • ~5 minutes per presentation + 2-3 minutes for group feedback
  • Present to your triad group
  • Peer evaluation completed immediately after all presentations
  • Upload your slides to Canvas during or after class
