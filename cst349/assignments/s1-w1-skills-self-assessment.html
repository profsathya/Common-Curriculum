2 Rate Yourself on 10 Skills
‚ñº

Below are 10 "beyond technical" skills. For each one, rate yourself honestly on two dimensions:

  • Current level (1-5): Where you actually are right now
  • Needed level (1-5): Where you need to be for your goals

1. Clear Communication

Explaining complex ideas simply; writing that people actually read

2. Active Listening

Understanding what others actually mean, not just what they say

3. Conflict Navigation

Handling disagreements without avoiding them or escalating them

4. Emotional Regulation

Staying composed under stress; managing frustration and anxiety

5. Giving Feedback

Being honest without being harsh; making feedback actionable

6. Receiving Feedback

Hearing criticism without defensiveness; extracting value from it

7. Presence/Confidence

Coming across as competent; speaking up in rooms where you feel junior

8. Time/Priority Management

Focusing on what matters; saying no to what doesn't

9. Networking/Relationship Building

Building genuine professional connections; asking for help

10. Adaptability

Responding to unexpected changes without spiraling; pivoting gracefully
