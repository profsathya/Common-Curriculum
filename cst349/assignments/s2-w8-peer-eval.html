THREE LENSES FOR WATCHING DEMOS
NOTICE: What specific evidence of growth did they show? Before/after? "I saw..." / "They showed..." / "Their evidence was..."
INTERPRET: Was the evidence behavioral and verifiable, or a feeling/claim? "This suggests..." / "The gap between their claim and evidence is..." "The before/after shows..."
ASK: What question would sharpen or test their evidence? "What would someone outside your group say about this evidence?" "If you removed the AI-assisted parts, what growth remains?" "What changed between Sprint 1 and now — and how do you know?"
HOW TO ASK A HARD QUESTION WITHOUT BEING A JERK: 1. Clarify: "Help me understand..." 2. Value: "I noticed you did ___, which makes sense because..." 3. Then ask: "The thing I'm curious about is..."
