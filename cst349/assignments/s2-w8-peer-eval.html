Peer Evaluation

Evaluate your group members · Wednesday, March 11 (in class)

After watching your group members' demos, evaluate each person's evidence of growth and their contribution to the group. Be honest and specific — your feedback helps them and informs the instructor's grading.

For Each Group Member

Complete the following evaluation for each member of your triad (not yourself).

Demo Quality

How convincing was their evidence of growth? (1-5)

5 = undeniable evidence, clear before/after, external validation. 1 = vague claims, no real evidence.

Demo Quality

What was the strongest piece of evidence in their demo?

Point to something specific. What made you believe they actually grew?

Demo Quality

What was missing or unconvincing?

Be constructive. What would have made their demo stronger?

Group Contribution

How did this person contribute to YOUR growth? (1-5)

5 = significantly helped me grow through feedback, accountability, support. 1 = no meaningful contribution to my growth.

Group Contribution

Give a specific example of how they helped (or didn't help) the group.

What did they actually do? Be concrete.

Honesty Matters

Your evaluations are confidential. Giving everyone a 5 doesn't help anyone. Honest feedback — even when it's tough — is how groups improve. Your evaluations contribute to the Check-in/Engagement grade.

What to Submit

Complete the evaluation for each group member during class. Submit individually.
