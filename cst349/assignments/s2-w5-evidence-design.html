Good Evidence vs. Weak Evidence

Strong Evidence

  • Before/after comparison
  • External feedback (group partners, others)
  • Specific examples with dates
  • Measurable change

Weak Evidence

  • "I feel like I improved"
  • Effort without results
  • Vague claims with no specifics
  • Only self-assessment
