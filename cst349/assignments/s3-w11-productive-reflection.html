Productive Reflection #9 SDL

🪞 Reflection · Individual · Due

Reflection Prompt

You've been consuming new sources for two weeks now. The question isn't whether you read something — it's whether what you read changed anything you actually do or think.

Answer honestly:

1

What are you actually doing differently because of what you've been consuming? Not plans — what have you already changed?

2

If the answer is "nothing yet," say that and say why. What's the gap between consuming and changing?

3

What's one thing you believe differently now about your focus area compared to Week 9? If your thinking hasn't shifted, what would it take to shift it?

Format: Handwritten or typed — your choice. If handwritten, photograph and upload. Aim for genuine reflection, not length. A focused half-page is worth more than two pages of filler.
