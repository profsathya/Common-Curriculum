Look-back 5: Era Extra Credit

Sprint 4 · Unlocks Week 14 · Due May 23, 2026

The agency through-line

This one is different.

The first four look-backs each asked about one moment. This one asks about the pattern across them. Read all four of your own answers together and look at the shape. The question isn't whether the course was good or bad. The question is whether you showed up for it.

Prerequisite: Look-backs 1 through 4 must all be completed and credited first. This one looks across them.

Pull up your four look-back responses

Gather your written answers to Look-backs 1, 2, 3, and 4. Read them in order, all in one sitting. Don't edit. Don't defend. Just notice.

Answer these questions

Read your answers to look-backs 1 through 4 together. What pattern do you see across them? What do they tell you about whether you've been taking responsibility for your own learning in this course — not whether the course was good or bad, but whether you showed up for it? What's the most uncomfortable thing your own answers reveal? There's no wrong answer here, but there's an honest one.

How to earn credit

Write your response. Bring it to an in-class check-in with the instructor — your work is reviewed and credited on the spot. Then continue to the paired Commons conversation on the platform.
