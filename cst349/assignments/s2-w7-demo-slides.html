Demo Slide Builder

Build your 5-slide demo through AI-guided discussion · Due Monday, March 9

Your Sprint 2 demo is Wednesday. This activity walks you through building each slide of your presentation with AI-guided reflection. By the end, you'll have the content for all 5 slides.

The AI will push you to be specific, honest, and evidence-based. Vague claims get follow-up questions. Strong evidence gets refined.

The 5-Slide Template

  1. The quality & why it matters
  2. Your starting point
  3. What you did
  4. Evidence of growth
  5. Your trajectory / what's next
1 The Quality & Why It Matters

Name the specific soft skill or quality you've been developing. Why does it matter — not in general, but to YOU and your career?

The AI will ask: Why this quality specifically? How does it connect to your goals? Why should your audience care?
2 Your Starting Point

Where were you at the beginning of Sprint 2? Be honest about your baseline. Use the evidence you designed in Week 5.

The AI will ask: How do you know that was your starting point? What evidence do you have of your baseline? What did others observe?
3 What You Did

What specific actions did you take? Not just "I practiced" — what exactly, when, how often? What did your group help with?

The AI will ask: Can your group verify these actions? What was the hardest thing you did? What did you try that didn't work?
4 Evidence of Growth

This is the heart of your demo. Show before/after comparisons, external feedback, concrete examples with dates. What would convince a skeptic?

The AI will ask: Is this evidence of growth or just effort? What would someone who doesn't know you think? Can your group validate this?
5 Your Trajectory / What's Next

Where are you headed? What's your honest assessment of where you are now and where you're going? What will you continue working on?

The AI will ask: What's the gap between where you are and where you want to be? What's your plan for closing it? What did this sprint teach you about how you grow?

How This Works

This is an AI-discussion activity. For each slide, you'll:

  1. Write your initial content for the slide
  2. Get AI follow-up questions pushing for specificity and evidence
  3. Refine your response based on the follow-ups
  4. Move to the next slide

Your refined responses become the content for your actual demo slides.
