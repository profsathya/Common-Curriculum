Networking Event Report

Document your networking experience · Due

The networking event is over. Now turn your experience into evidence you can use in your demo. What did you learn from professionals that validates (or challenges) your growth?

Your Report

1

Who did you talk to?

List 2-3 people you had meaningful conversations with. What are their roles? What's their background?

2

What did you learn about your skill in a professional context?

How do professionals think about the skill you're developing? Was their perspective different from yours? What surprised you?

3

What feedback did you receive?

Did anyone comment on your approach, your questions, or your understanding? Any advice you received?

4

How does this connect to your demo evidence?

What from this event can you use as evidence in Slide 4 (evidence of growth) or Slide 5 (trajectory)? Be specific.

5

Follow-up actions

Did you send thank-you messages? Make LinkedIn connections? What follow-up have you done or will you do?

What to Submit

Your networking event report covering all sections above. Include any evidence you gathered (screenshots of follow-up messages, notes, etc.).
