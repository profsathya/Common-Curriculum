Design Philosophy

Why the meeting is structured this way

Experience before explanation: The team needs to experience working with AI at a higher level of abstraction before they can understand the framework we're trying to teach students. The original agenda had abstract discussions; the new design puts hands-on experience first.

Small group activities: The entire meeting is designed around paired and small group work. This creates ownership through participation, not just observation.

Minimal worksheets: This is a mature team that doesn't need prescriptive guidance. The structure provides scaffolding; the team provides the thinking.
