Revised Plan AB

Sprint 3 · Due · 5 points

📅 Due during spring break (April 5). Intentionally short — 100 words. Should take 15–20 minutes.

~1 min · Why this matters

This is intentionally short. You already have a plan — you're adjusting it based on evidence. The skill here is conciseness: can you name exactly what's changing and why in 100 words?

— Prof. Sathya

What to Submit

Two things, clearly labeled. 100 words max. That's it — this is a revision note, not a new plan.

  1. What's changing from v1 to v2 and why. Be specific — not "making it better" but "changing from a multi-page flow to a single-screen checklist because the self-test showed my user won't tap through pages in a 3-second window."
  2. What feedback drove this change. Was it the self-test, the AI challenge, something Piranavan said, your own reflection? Name the source.
