This Week's Peer Skill: How to Understand

Get into someone else's perspective, listen before reacting, surface what's behind their thinking.

The Conversation Context

In class, you explored these prompts with your peer partner:

(1) Think of a conversation where two people clearly weren't understanding each other. What was actually happening?

(2) What's the bottleneck when conversations fail?

(3) When does it work for YOU? What do you need to feel heard?

This reflection helps you consolidate what you learned from the conversation and identify how to apply it going forward.
