Look-back 2: One AI Interaction Extra Credit

Sprint 4 · Unlocks Week 14 · Due May 23, 2026

Why this look-back

Every prompt is a judgment call — this look-back asks what you were optimizing for at one specific moment.

Prerequisite: Look-back 1 (Problem Selection) must be completed and credited first.

Pull up one AI interaction log

Find one AI conversation from Sprint 1 or Sprint 2 — from any tool (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) or from your Symbiotic Thinking Dojo logs. Pick one where you can still see what you typed, not just what the AI said back.

It doesn't have to be a long conversation. One exchange is enough if you can see the prompt-by-prompt judgment calls.

Answer these questions

Pick one AI interaction from Sprint 1 or 2 — either from a tool like ChatGPT/Claude, or from the Symbiotic Thinking Dojo. Read what you actually typed. At what point in the conversation did you decide what to ask next? What were you choosing between? The moment you typed "write me…" instead of "help me think about…" was a judgment call. Find one of those moments. What were you optimizing for in that moment — speed, comfort, the grade, your own thinking? Was that the right thing to optimize for?

How to earn credit

Write your response. Bring it to an in-class check-in with the instructor — your work is reviewed and credited on the spot. Then continue to the paired Commons conversation on the platform.
