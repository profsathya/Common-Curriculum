Solution Brief AB

Sprint 3 · Due · 10 points

This goes to Piranavan and the Bhutan clinicians. Write it for them, not for me. They should be able to read this in 2 minutes and understand what you built, why, and what is still missing.

— Prof. Sathya

What to Submit

Four parts, clearly labeled. ~300 words total.

  1. Who and What (~50 words). Name the person your solution is for, their role, and the specific situation. Not the general problem — the specific moment.
  2. What Your Solution Does (~100 words). Walk through the experience from the user's perspective. What do they see, do, and get? Describe the experience, not the tech stack.
  3. One Key Decision (~75 words). Name one design decision you made and why. What was the alternative you didn't choose, and what would be worse about your solution if you had?
  4. What's Still Unfinished (~75 words). What doesn't your solution handle yet? Be specific — name the gaps, not "it needs more work."
