Look-back 5: Era Extra Credit

Sprint 4 · Unlocks Week 14 · Due May 23, 2026

The agency through-line

This one is different.

The first four look-backs each asked about one judgment call. This one asks about the pattern of calls you made across all of them. The work is yours either way — but were you the one making the judgments inside it, or was something else?

Prerequisite: Look-backs 1 through 4 must all be completed and credited first. This one looks across them.

Pull up your four look-back responses

Gather your written answers to Look-backs 1, 2, 3, and 4. Read them in order, all in one sitting. Don't edit. Don't defend. Just notice.

Answer these questions

Read your answers to look-backs 1 through 4 together. Across these four moments, what does the pattern of your judgment calls reveal about you? Were you consistently choosing the harder, more honest option, or consistently choosing the easier, faster, less exposing one? What's the most uncomfortable thing about how you made these calls? And here's the harder question: the work was yours either way — but were YOU the one making the judgments inside it, or was something else (the deadline, the rubric, what you thought the instructor wanted) making them for you?

How to earn credit

Write your response. Bring it to an in-class check-in with the instructor — your work is reviewed and credited on the spot. Then continue to the paired Commons conversation on the platform.

Window: Look-back assignments unlock sequentially starting Week 14. The final look-back is due May 23, 2026.
