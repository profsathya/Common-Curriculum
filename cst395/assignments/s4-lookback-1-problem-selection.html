Look-back 1: Problem Selection Extra Credit

Sprint 4 · Unlocks Week 14 · Due May 23, 2026

Why this look-back

The problem you chose shaped everything that followed — this look-back asks whether you chose it, or just avoided the harder ones.

Prerequisite: None. This is the first look-back in the sequence.

Pull up your Sprint 1 or Sprint 2 problem

Open Canvas → Sprint 1 Superagency Challenge problem statement or Sprint 2 Discovery Doc / partner-need statement. Your choice — one of the two. Look at the problem you actually committed to.

Answer these questions

Look at the problem you chose in Sprint 1 (the thing you wanted to change about your own life) OR the partner-need you chose to address in Sprint 2 — student choice. What were the other options you considered, and why did you rule them out? If you didn't seriously consider alternatives, that itself is the judgment call worth examining. Was your choice an act of selection, or an act of avoidance? What would a harder choice have asked of you?

How to earn credit

Write your response. Bring it to an in-class check-in with the instructor — your work is reviewed and credited on the spot. Then continue to the paired Commons conversation on the platform.
