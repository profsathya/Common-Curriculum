Design Rationale Log AB SDL

Sprint 2 · Due Saturday, March 7 · 5 points

A hiring committee doesn't just want to see what you built — they want to see HOW you thought.

This activity walks you through documenting your design decisions in a way that shows your reasoning process.

How This Works

For each major design decision, the AI challenges your reasoning:

The AI will push you with moves like:

  • "You chose X over Y — what would have to be true for Y to be the better choice?"
  • "Where did AI help with this decision vs. where did your judgment override it?"
  • "How did your partner's feedback influence this choice? If it didn't, why not?"
  • "If your riskiest assumption turns out to be wrong, which decisions break?"

What You'll Produce

For each major design decision:

  1. What you decided
  2. Alternatives you considered
  3. Why this choice (with reasoning, not just preference)
  4. AI's role vs. your judgment
  5. How partner feedback influenced it

Interactive Activity

The interactive activity will guide you through documenting each design decision. For now, start thinking about the key decisions you made while building your prototype.

Complete the interactive activity and download your responses as JSON.
