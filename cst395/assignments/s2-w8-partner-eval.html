THREE LENSES FOR WATCHING DEMOS
NOTICE: What specifically did they do or show? "I saw..." / "They showed..." / "The partner said..."
INTERPRET: What does that tell you? "This suggests..." / "The gap between X and Y is..." "They chose ___ over ___ because..."
ASK: What would make them think? "What tradeoff did you make between ___ and ___?" "What changed after your partner told you ___?" "What would you see if this worked? If it didn't?" "What was the version you almost built instead?" "What would your partner say is the part that matters most — and is that what you focused on?"
HOW TO ASK A HARD QUESTION WITHOUT BEING A JERK: 1. Clarify: "Help me understand..." 2. Value: "I noticed you did ___, which makes sense because..." 3. Then ask: "The thing I'm curious about is..."
