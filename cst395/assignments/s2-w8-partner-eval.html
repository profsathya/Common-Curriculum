Partner Evaluation IS

Sprint 2 · Completed in class on Demo Day, March 11 · 5 points

In-Class Activity

This evaluation is completed in class on Demo Day (Wednesday, March 11) after watching your partner's demo presentation.

This is the ultimate Sprint 2 evidence: your partner's own assessment of whether you understood them.

Be honest — this helps both of you learn.

How This Works

The AI pushes beyond generic positive feedback:

The AI will push you with moves like:

  • "You rated their understanding 4/5 — what's missing for a 5? Be specific."
  • "You said the solution 'addressed your need.' But is there a difference between what you asked for and what you actually needed? Did they get the deeper thing?"
  • "What surprised you about their approach — something they saw about your situation that you didn't see yourself?"

What You'll Submit (Evaluating Your Partner)

  1. Did your partner understand your problem? (Rate 1-5 + explain)
  2. Did the solution address your actual need or your stated want? What's the difference?
  3. What surprised you about their approach — something they saw that you didn't see yourself?
  4. Would you use what they built? Why or why not?
  5. What's one thing they did that showed genuine understanding of you as a person, not just your problem?
