Challenge Revision

You've made a decision about your challenge. Now make it count.

This is only for students who chose to Reframe or Replace.

Context

In Week 2, you assessed your Superagency Challenge against the Sprint 1 criteria: Is it about YOU? Are you treating it as a design problem (not a willpower problem)?

Your Decision

Keep

My challenge is personal AND I'm treating it as a design problem

No submission needed

Reframe

My challenge is personal but I need to shift from willpower to design thinking

Submit revision

Replace

My challenge isn't about me — I need to choose something personal

Submit revision

Submission

What to Submit

If you chose REFRAME:

  • Original framing: What was your original challenge?
  • Willpower trap: How were you treating it as a willpower problem?
  • Design reframe: How are you now framing it as a design problem?
  • What changes: What's different about how you'll approach this?

If you chose REPLACE:

  • Why replace: Why wasn't your original challenge about you?
  • New challenge: What personal challenge are you choosing instead?
  • Why it matters: Why does this challenge matter to you?
  • Design approach: How will you treat this as a design problem (not willpower)?

Format: Text entry · Due: Friday, January 23

Before submitting: Is your (revised) challenge about YOU? Are you treating it as a design problem?
