Sprint 2 Goal Setting

Two-part assignment · Part I due Monday, Feb 16 · Part II due Thursday, Feb 20

Sprint 2 pairs you with a classmate. You'll help solve their problem — and they'll help you understand yours from a new perspective.

The hardest part isn't building. It's truly understanding the problem from another person's point of view.

This assignment has two parts: preparation (before you meet your partner) and goal-setting (after you meet them in class).

Part I: Preparation (Due Monday, Feb 16)

Before you know who your partner is, prepare yourself. This AI-guided activity helps you reflect on your problem-solving skills and draft questions for your first "customer conversation."
