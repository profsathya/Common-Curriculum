Human Value Statement

SDL IS

The Human Value Proposition

Throughout this sprint, you've worked with AI to solve a problem for yourself. But you weren't just a passenger ‚Äî you provided judgment, direction, and taste that shaped the outcome. This statement articulates exactly what YOU contributed.

"What would be worse about my solution if I had simply handed the problem to AI?"

What to Include

  1. Specific decisions you made ‚Äî Where did you override AI suggestions? Where did you choose one direction over another?
  2. Judgment you applied ‚Äî What did you know about yourself or your context that AI couldn't have known?
  3. The counterfactual ‚Äî What would the solution look like (or not exist) if you had just asked AI to solve it?
  4. Why it matters ‚Äî How did your involvement make the solution actually work for you?
Example excerpt

"AI suggested I needed a comprehensive habit tracking system with streaks, reminders, and analytics. But from my 5 Whys, I knew my real problem was work-boundary anxiety, not motivation. I directed the solution toward a simple 'work is done' ritual instead. The judgment I provided: knowing that more tracking would increase my anxiety, not reduce it. Without this, I'd have another app I'd abandon in two weeks."
