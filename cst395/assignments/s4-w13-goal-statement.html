How this works

Six steps. The same flow you'll use for every Sprint 4 form activity.

  1. Complete the form below. Pick your path (solo or pair-merge), then fill in all the required fields. Ground every answer in your Sprint 3 Solution Brief.
  2. Review your responses. Re-read each section before submitting. Be honest — vague goals can be sent back for revision.
  3. Export as markdown. Enter your name at the bottom of the activity and click Download Responses. The JSON download includes a markdown field with your full Goal Statement.
  4. Paste into a Google Doc. Create a new Google Doc titled "[Your name] — Sprint 4 Goal Statement" and paste the markdown in.
  5. Download as PDF. File → Download → PDF Document.
  6. Submit the PDF to Canvas. Upload the PDF on the Canvas assignment page — nothing else.
