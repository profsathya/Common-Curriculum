2The 3Cs Framework
‚ñº

When you work with AI, use the 3Cs to structure every interaction:

C
Context
What does AI need to know?
C
Choices
What options should AI consider?
C
Confirmation
How will you verify the output?

Why this matters

AI is good at generating options. You're good at judgment. 3Cs keeps you in control of the direction while leveraging AI's capacity.

3Design Your Solution
‚ñº

Now sketch out your solution. Answer these questions:

  1. The human process: What behavior or habit needs to change? Describe it without mentioning any technology.
  2. The friction points: Where in that process do you typically fail or give up?
  3. Where tech helps: What specific friction points could technology reduce?
  4. Your solution sketch: What will you actually build or create?

‚è∏Ô∏è Important: Write down your answers to all 4 questions before continuing. This is your solution architecture.
