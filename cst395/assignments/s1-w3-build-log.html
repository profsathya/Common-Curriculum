What's a Build Log?

A build log captures the decisions you make while building‚Äînot just what you did, but why you did it, what else you considered, and what you learned.

Why this matters: When you reflect on your Human Value at the end of the sprint, you'll need evidence of the judgment you applied. The build log is that evidence.

What to Document

Each time you make a significant choice, capture these:

What I decided

The choice you made (be specific)

What I considered

Other options you thought about

Why this one

Your reasoning for choosing this path

What I learned

Anything surprising or new you discovered

Example Entry

Entry #3 ‚Äî Tuesday 2pm
What I decided: Focus on just the morning routine, not the whole day
What I considered: Full day tracking, morning + evening, morning only
Why this one: My 5 Whys showed the problem is starting tasks. Mornings are when that's worst. Keep scope small.
What I learned: AI kept suggesting full-day solutions. I had to push back based on my own self-knowledge.

üí° Tip: You don't need to document everything. Focus on moments where you made a real choice‚Äîespecially where you disagreed with AI or chose against the "obvious" path.
