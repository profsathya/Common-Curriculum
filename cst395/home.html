AI-Native Solution Engineering

Becoming an Architect.

Your goal is Superagency. Your method is Symbiotic Thinking.

This isn't a course about using AI tools. It's a course about becoming someone who can solve problems that were previously beyond their reach ‚Äî using AI as a cognitive partner.

We call this Superagency: the ability to attempt problems you wouldn't have tackled before. But Superagency without Human Value is just relying on the tool. You'll learn to identify and apply where your judgment, taste, and direction matter ‚Äî and demonstrate it with evidence.

By semester end, you'll answer two questions with evidence:

  • "What problems are now within my reach that I would not have attempted before?"
  • "What would be worse about my solutions if I had simply handed the problems to AI?"
