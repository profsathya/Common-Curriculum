What’s actually happening in the market

The market is already different.

84% of employers want to see the work, not read about it. (2026 employer surveys)

70% of employers use skills-based hiring for entry-level roles. (NACE 2026)

The era of “spray and pray” applications is over. Referral and portfolio-driven applications convert at 5-10× the rate of cold applies.

Translation: a resume is not enough anymore. The artifact you can show in 30 seconds is what gets you the conversation.

Two ways to stand out

The technical-demo bar — and the real-world judgment bar.

The technical-demo bar

  • Working app, clean UI
  • Live deployment
  • Stack cohesion (deep in 2-3 technologies)
  • Bar: does it work?
  • Strong for tutorial-built portfolios

The real-world judgment bar

  • A problem that mattered to a real stakeholder
  • Architectural and ethical decisions that show your thinking
  • Honest reflection on what you’d do differently
  • Evidence of working through ambiguity and human stakes
  • Bar: did you understand it deeply? Did you bring judgment that AI couldn’t?

You’ve been working at the second bar all semester. Most students never get there. Your portfolio just has to make that visible to people who don’t already know your work.

The ledger

What it costs you, what you get.

What it costs

  • 3-5 hours of focused polish upfront — mostly packaging existing work for an external audience
  • ~2 hours per week through the summer to iterate based on feedback you receive
  • A public repo under your name (worth thinking about — not trivial for everyone)
  • Willingness to receive direct, sometimes uncomfortable feedback from industry reviewers

What you get

  • A live, durable artifact you control — lives past CSUMB, past your first job, past your second
  • My continued support through the summer — I’m not handing this off in May. We’ll iterate together.
  • Industry friends I’m bringing in will leave real engineering feedback, not platitudes
  • A URL you can drop in any application, message, or conversation that says “this is how I think and build”

This is opt-in. If you’re job-hunting or internship-hunting this summer, this is one of the highest-leverage things you can do with that time. If you’re not, your defense and brief stand on their own.
