The Challenge Session

Watch how to challenge assumptions productively. Then do it with your partner.

Challenge Moves

  • Present your understanding: "Here's what I think your problem is..."
  • Partner pushes back: "That's close, but you're missing..."
  • Test assumptions: "I assumed X because you said Y. Am I right?"
  • Find the gap: Where does your understanding break down?

Demo Design

How Will You Show Your Process?

Sprint 2's demo isn't a feature tour. It's showing how you understood someone.

Demo Structure Preview

  1. "Here's my partner and the problem I discovered."
  2. "Here's what I learned that they didn't tell me directly."
  3. "Here's what I built and the key decisions I made."
  4. "Here's where I was wrong and what I changed."
  5. "Here's what would be worse if I'd just handed this to AI."

Present as if a hiring committee asked you: "Show us how you solved a problem for someone."
