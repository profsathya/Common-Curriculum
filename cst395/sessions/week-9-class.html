The Work Begins

Who is your user? What is the moment? Stake it now.

1. What does your user probably do right now?

Not what you think they should do. What do they actually do when they hit this moment — with the tools, time, and resources they actually have?

2. What would a better human process look like?

Forget technology. If you could redesign how this person handles this moment — the steps, the information flow, the decision points — what would be different? Describe the process, not a product.

Design the human process first. Technology is a later question.
