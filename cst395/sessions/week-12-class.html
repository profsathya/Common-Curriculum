Partner Discussion · 8 Steps
1
Answer the questions — handwritten, on paperNo typing. No AI. Closed book.
2
Find a partner and swap your written responsesYou'll work with their answers, they'll work with yours.
3
Enter your partner's responses in the S3 Demo Discussion formTranscribe their handwriting faithfully — don't edit.
4
Come up with follow-up questions — as many as you canQFT rules: don't stop, don't judge, write them all down.
5
Use AI's help if you need it — optionalAI can surface angles you missed. It doesn't replace your thinking.
6
Ask your questions and have a real conversationFace-to-face. No notes. Listen for their best thinking.
7
Swap roles — let your partner ask you questionsYour turn to defend your thinking out loud.
8
Return the written paper to the instructorYour handwritten responses are the artifact of record.
