Week 8 · Sprint 2 Demo Day

Demo Day

Show how you solved a problem for someone. Your partner decides if you understood them.

Logistics

How Today Works

Demo Format

  • Two groups of 5 pairs — each group presents together
  • Both partners present: you show what you built, your partner validates
  • 5 minutes per presentation — follow the demo structure
  • Partner evaluation completed after each presentation

This is not a feature demo. It's a story about understanding someone.

Demo Structure

Your 5 Minutes

Present in This Order

  1. "Here's my partner and the problem I discovered."
    Brief context — who they are, what you found.
  2. "Here's what I learned that they didn't tell me directly."
    Show the depth of your understanding.
  3. "Here's what I built and the key decisions I made."
    Solution + rationale, not a feature tour.
  4. "Here's where I was wrong and what I changed."
    Iteration from feedback.
  5. "Here's what would be worse if I'd handed this to AI."
    Your human value.
