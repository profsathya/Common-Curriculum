Before We Start

How to Give Useful Feedback

As the Partner Being Shown a Prototype

  • Don't be polite. "It's nice" helps no one. Say what's actually true.
  • Point to specifics. "This part feels right because..." or "This misses the mark because..."
  • Distinguish want vs. need. "I asked for X, but what I actually need is Y"
  • Notice surprises. "I didn't expect you to include this — and it matters because..."

The best feedback isn't about the solution. It's about whether they understood the problem.
