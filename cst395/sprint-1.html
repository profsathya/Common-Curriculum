Sprint 1: Foundation

"Superagency Over Self" ‚Äî Building capability by solving your own problem

Weeks 1-4 High Scaffolding Stakeholder: Yourself

By Sprint End, You Will Have:

  • Identified something you've wanted to change but felt was beyond your capacity
  • Applied 5 Whys to find the real barrier (not just the surface symptom)
  • Designed and built a solution using AI as a cognitive partner
  • Articulated your specific Human Value contribution
  • Presented value (not features) to peers

Concepts: AI as cognitive partner (not tool). Superagency mindset: your limits are not fixed. Meta-habits: Slow Down, Know Yourself, Take the Lead.
