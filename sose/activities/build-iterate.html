Build → review → revise

Simple-and-working beats sophisticated-and-stuck.
Learning outcome
You can make progress under uncertainty by shipping something small that works and improving it.

The activity

The core build loop: each round gets something real in front of someone and teaches you the next move.

How to do it

  1. Build the smallest version that does something real.
  2. Get it in front of someone — maintainer, user, or peer — and review honestly.
  3. Revise based on what you learned, then run the loop again.
What to show
