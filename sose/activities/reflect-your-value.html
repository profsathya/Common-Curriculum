Reflect: where does your value concentrate?

Find your angle on a gap — what you can see that others can’t yet.
Learning outcome
You can name what you specifically can see or do here that others — including AI — can’t yet.

The activity

A by-hand reflection at the intersection of what you’ve learned and where you sit. This is where your gap gets personal.

How to reflect

  1. By hand, note where your background, the project’s needs, and the community’s blind spots overlap.
  2. Ask: what’s obvious to me that isn’t obvious here? What could I do that AI alone couldn’t?
  3. Use that to narrow which gap is yours to pursue.
