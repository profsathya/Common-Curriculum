Summer Open Source Experience

Find the Gap. Go For It.

Spot a real gap in an open-source project's ecosystem — and build the confidence to take it on.
Over eight weeks you go from fixing your first issue to finding a gap no one has taken on, and pursuing it. You learn the project fast, find the real need by talking to the people in it, and build toward it with AI and the community as partners — not autopilot.
