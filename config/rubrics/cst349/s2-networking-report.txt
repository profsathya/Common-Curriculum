Networking Event Report for CST349. Quality criteria:
- Does the report capture specific things professionals said (not vague summaries)?
- Is there a comparison between what the student assumed and what they heard?
- Are there specific moments of surprise or challenge to their assumptions?
- Does the student identify concrete takeaways that change their approach?
- Red flag: Generic summary ("it was great to network"), no specific quotes or insights, no assumption challenges.
- Green flag: Specific professional quotes or insights, honest description of assumptions that were wrong, concrete plans to adjust their demo or skill practice based on what they heard.
- Critical signal: Does the student confront the gap between self-assessment and external assessment?
