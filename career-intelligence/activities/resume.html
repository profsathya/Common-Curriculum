Before the session

Submit Your Current Resume

Learning goal

Your starting point on paper — the resume you will keep sharpening as your niche sharpens.

What to do

  1. Put your current resume into a Google Doc. As-is is fine — no need to polish or rewrite before submitting.
  2. Set sharing so anyone with the link can view.
  3. Keep updating this same doc every week of the program. Don't make a new copy each time — the version history is part of the point.

Submit to Canvas

Submission type: Website URL

What to submit: Paste the Google Doc URL into Canvas.
