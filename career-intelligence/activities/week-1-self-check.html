Week 1 Self-Check

Learning goal

Check your own week. Confirm the artifacts are in place, and notice where the thinking is solid versus where it’s still vague. The work is yours to know — this check is what makes that real.

What to do

  1. Review on your own first. Walk back through this week’s artifacts:
    • Handwritten Know Yourself reflection (page + typed copy)
    • Initial Know Yourself Dojo JSON
    • Updated Know Yourself Dojo JSON
    • Know the Market research + Dojo JSON
    • Niche Doc start
  2. Run it past AI for a quick gut-check — ask where the thinking is thin or generic. Use what’s useful, ignore what isn’t.
  3. Verify with a human. Show your Niche Doc start to a peer, TA, or instructor and ask what they hear. AI alone is not a check — a person will catch what AI smooths over.

Submit to Canvas

Submission type: Text entry

What to submit: A short note (3–5 sentences) with three things:

  • What feels solid — specific, your own, something you’d defend
  • What feels shaky — generic, borrowed, or you’re not sure you believe it yet
  • One thing you want to revisit next week
