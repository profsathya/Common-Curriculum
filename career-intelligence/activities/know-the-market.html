Know the Market — Research + Dojo JSON

Learning goal

Get a real read on what the market actually needs — from real postings and real people, not from vibes or AI summaries.

What to do

  1. Collect 3–5 real job postings in an area you’re curious about. Real postings from real companies — not generic role descriptions.
  2. Note the recurring skills and requirements across them. What shows up in every one? What shows up in some? What surprised you?
  3. Look up 2–3 people who currently hold roles like that (LinkedIn is fine). Note their paths into the role — what they did before, when they pivoted, what they have in common.
  4. Open the Know the Market Dojo at the link below and work through it with your research in front of you. Download the JSON at the end.
