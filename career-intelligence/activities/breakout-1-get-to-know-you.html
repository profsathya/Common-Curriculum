Breakout 1 — Get to Know You (Trios)

Learning goal

Meet your trio. Get past name and major into what you actually care about — you’ll be giving and receiving feedback from these people for the rest of the program.

What to do

In class · ~12 minutes · trios of 3.

  1. One person makes a copy of the Google Doc below and shares it with the trio (anyone-with-link can edit).
  2. Rotate through three roles. In each round one person asks, one answers, one takes notes. Keep going until everyone has done each role.
  3. Use the get-to-know-you questions in the doc. Have fun — this is the easy one.
